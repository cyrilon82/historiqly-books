```
TOPIC: [Name]

PRIMARY KEYWORD: "[main search term]"
SECONDARY KEYWORD: "[alternative term]"
QUESTION KEYWORD: "[How/What/Who/Where question]"
ANGLE MODIFIER: "[unique hook or perspective]"
UPSTREAM LINKS: [2 related topics that link TO this]
DOWNSTREAM LINKS: [2 related topics this links TO]
```
